description=[h2]New features[/h2]
description=- Volvo FE6 Dump Truck model (courtesy of fhqwhgads from Motorius Zone!)
description=- Improved gravel dumping logic
description=- 3-tile wide roads
description=
description=[h2]Features[/h2]
description=- Erosion prevention: gravel roads won't erode or grow grass or trees over time
description=- Automatic edge blending: gravel roads blend smoothly with grass and terrain
description=- Smart corner filling: gaps between angled roads automatically fill in
description=- Realistic spawn variation: 50% chance trucks spawn with 80-100% gravel load
description=
description=[h2]Usage[/h2]
description=1. Find a Dump Truck (spawns in construction, farm, and industrial zones)
description=2. Load gravel sacks into the truck bed (if empty or low)
description=3. Press (G) or use the vehicle radial menu to start dumping gravel
description=4. (Optional) Toggle road width between 2 and 3 tiles via radial menu
description=5. Drive slowly (5 km/h max while dumping) to create a gravel road
description=6. Press (G) or use the radial menu again to stop dumping
description=
