title=Road Builder
description=[b]Supports Multiplayer[/b]
description=Build gravel roads to connect your base to town, create safe supply routes, or carve paths through the wilderness. Roads that look like they belong in the game — smooth edge blending, erosion prevention, and perfectly straight lines when you need them.
description=
description=[h2]Features[/h2]
description=- Volvo FE6 dump truck model (courtesy of [b]fhqwhgads[/b] from [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2791656602]Motorious Zone[/url]!)
description=- Erosion prevention: gravel roads won't erode or grow grass or trees over time
description=- Automatic edge blending: gravel roads blend smoothly with grass and terrain
description=- Snap Line: build perfectly straight roads with one click
description=
description=[h2]Usage[/h2]
description=1. Find a dump truck (50% spawn with gravel, 50% with empty sacks)
description=2. Load gravel sacks into the truck bed (if empty or low)
description=3. (Optional) Toggle road width between 2 and 3 tiles in the radial menu (v)
description=4. Raise the bed via radial menu to start dumping
description=5. Drive slowly (5 km/h max while dumping) to create a gravel road
description=6. Lower the bed via radial menu, or it stops automatically when empty
description=
description=[h2]Straight Roads (Snap Line)[/h2]
description=Want a perfectly straight road? Use Snap Line to snap gravel placement to a dead-straight line.
description=
description=1. Point the truck roughly North, South, East, or West
description=2. Open the radial menu (V) and click [b]Enable Snap Line[/b] — the label shows which direction it will lock to
description=3. Start dumping — gravel will land in a perfectly straight line no matter how much the truck wobbles
description=
description=[b]Snap Line automatically disengages when you:[/b]
description=- Hit the brakes
description=- Drift more than 3 tiles off the locked line
description=- Toggle it off in the radial menu
description=
description=When it disengages, dumping stops too — so you always start fresh. Works great with the 3-tile wide road setting for highway-style roads.
description=
description=[h2]Spawn Locations[/h2]
description=Where to find dump trucks (sorted most to least likely):
description=- Trades (construction and trade areas)

description=- Farms (agricultural areas)
description=- Junkyards (scrapyards and junkyards)
description=- Traffic Jams (roadblocks and traffic zones)
description=- Parking Stalls (general parking lots) - very low chance
description=- Medium Areas (medium density zones) - very low chance
