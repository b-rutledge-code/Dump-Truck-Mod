description=[h2]Usage[/h2]
description=1. Find a dump truck (50% spawn with gravel, 50% with empty sacks)
description=2. Load gravel sacks into the truck bed (if empty or low)
description=3. (Optional) Toggle road width between 2 and 3 tiles in the radial menu (v)
description=4. Raise the bed via radial menu to start dumping
description=5. Drive slowly (5 km/h max while dumping) to create a gravel road
description=6. Lower the bed via radial menu, or it stops automatically when empty
